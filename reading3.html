Sarccharine
Sweet

Im not saccharine sweet. Im not one for prose and monologues but it lunges out of me. Like the buzzing of bees beneath of heart, i cant help it.
I hold myself back from it, honey overflowing out my mouth. I cant help but think its impolite to be so brazen. So open with my affections. Then i remember how brazen you are, and how could that possibly be wrong. How could your love possibly be wrong. How could mine, by extension?
You teach me a lot, through virtue of being. You mean so much to me
