Wished for Someone

sometimes i think something is out there, a deity of some kind. im not superstitious, nor am i religious but when you talk to me so warmly and when you hold my hands so gently i remember begging once, for a person who knows me, for a person who talks like the ocean and sings like the skies, for a person who looks at me. i remember being 6 years old and wanting nothing more than a friend. even after all these years, after all the ppl ive met, ive never met someone like you. its like the person in my mind came to life under brush strokes and murky skies.
