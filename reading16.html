Wanderlust

Wanderlust is such a fun word, isnt it. A desire for adventure so carnal and instinctual that it can only be described as a Lust. A sin, this magnificent thing that society tells stories about and imitates, the chance to explore and understand and experience. This immutable human desire, taken to such an extreme that you almost can’t help yourself, like youre drunk on it, hooked.
It’s how i feel about her, that nebulous space of being at once, something wholly familiar and almost base in it’s existence. Something that you can look at and Know that it belongs. And at the same time, the very concept of it is unknowable, the very existence of it impossible to fully grasp and predict.
Loving her is like feeling wanderlust. These burst of energies and the fantasies of what could be on the other side, of what lies ahead. Sometimes I fear I treat it like el Dorado, so impossibly grandiose that the reality could never fully live up to it, I fear I may put her on a pedestal so far that I can only worship her from the base.
